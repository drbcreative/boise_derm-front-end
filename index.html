MEET DR. BROOKS

Dr. Naomi Brooks is a board-certified dermatologist with more than 17 years experience treating many different medical and cosmetic dermatological conditions. Her patients are all ages, looking for a dermatologist who will provide custom care to fit their unique needs.

She came to Boise after 12 years as an army dermatologist, serving in South Korea; Washington, DC; Tacoma, Washington; North Carolina; and Baghdad, Iraq. She was chief of dermatology at Ft. Bragg, NC, and served as the dermatology consultant for the Coalition Health Forces in Iraq.
